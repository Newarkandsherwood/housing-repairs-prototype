Request a repair

You can use this service to report a non-emergency repair for your council home. You should only report one problem at a time.

If you need to report a repair for a leasehold property contact us via webchat or telephone 01636 650 000

Emergency Repairs

For emergency repairs see our emergency repairs page.

Gas leaks

If you suspect you have a gas leak, you must report it immediately to the Gas Emergency Service on 0800 111 999 or via textphone (minicom) on 0800 371 787
